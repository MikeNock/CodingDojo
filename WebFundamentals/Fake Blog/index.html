Why did Altcoins outperform Bitcoin?

To understand this, we must first understand bitcoin's delayed effect on altcoin markets.

  • Altcoins experience a delayed market reaction to bitcoin
  • This is by design as a volatily buffer
  • Despite this, altcoins experience their own volatility vectors
  • Massive influxes of trading volumes in short spurts give a 30min alert window to ride with the bulls.
